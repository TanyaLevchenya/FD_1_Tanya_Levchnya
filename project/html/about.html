our

history

Since 2017, the CAFEHOUSE bakery and confectionery has been constantly delighting you with fresh pastries and delicious coffee. We are constantly inventing new recipes for pastries, smoothies, cakes. Our confectioners undergo training abroad, which allows us to keep the quality of our products at the highest level. Visit us for delicious pastries.
Our

Confectionery

We have been open for you since 2017. Fresh pastries, delicious cakes, pies, pastries and muffins, aromatic coffee and a homely atmosphere. Come visit - or leave an order for a cake - to enjoy delicious pastries. The chef of our pastry shop is a real virtuoso of his craft, he manually creates extraordinary cakes, cookies, sorbets and sweets, decorated with berries and gold leaf. This is a great place for family tea, romantic date, meeting with friends or business negotiations.
